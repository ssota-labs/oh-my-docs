Domain documents are living product knowledge. Product contributors,
developers, and agents use the same stable terms and rules instead of inventing
local meanings in PRDs, plans, or code.

## Catalogs

- [Glossary](/docs/domain/glossary) — stable `TERM-*` meanings
- [Models](/docs/domain/models) — concepts, relationships, and lifecycle
- [Policies](/docs/domain/policies) — conditional product decisions and
  invariants

Specifications reference Domain IDs and define how those meanings and policies
are guaranteed by observable system behavior. They do not duplicate Domain
definitions.
